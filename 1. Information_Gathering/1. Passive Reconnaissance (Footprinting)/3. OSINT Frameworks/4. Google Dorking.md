# Google Dorking

## What is Google Dorking?
Google Dorking, also known as Google Hacking, is the practice of using advanced search operators to find information that is not readily visible on websites. This information is often publicly available but not linked.
  - Ethical Use: Security professionals, researchers, and penetration testers use it to find vulnerabilities in systems they own or have explicit permission to test. Journalists use it to find public data for stories.
  - nethical/Illegal Use: Using these techniques to access unauthorized data, exploit systems without permission, or steal information is illegal and unethical. You could face severe legal consequences.


## What are Google Dorks?
Google Dorks are specialized search queries that use advanced operators to refine a search. They help you pinpoint specific file types, search within specific websites, or find vulnerable data.
