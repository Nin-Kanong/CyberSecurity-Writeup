## What is GoPhish?
What is Gophish?

Gophish is a powerful, open-source phishing framework designed for businesses and security professionals to test their exposure to phishing attacks. It makes it easy to create, launch, and track phishing campaigns to educate employees and improve an organization's security posture.

### Key Features:
  - User-Friendly Web Interface: No command-line heavy lifting for daily operations.
  - Campaign Management: Easily setup and manage target groups.
  - Email Templating: Create convincing emails using a built-in HTML editor or by importing raw HTML.
  - Landing Pages: Design pages that mimic real login portals to capture credentials.
  - Tracking & Reporting: Track email opens, link clicks, and submitted data in real-time.
  - Sending Profiles: Configure your own email server (e.g., Office 365, Gmail, SMTP relay) to send emails.

- Your public IP (203.144.76.162) might change if:
  - You reboot your router
  - Your ISP assigns a new IP
  - You move networks
Without the DUC, your domain would point to an old IP → GoPhish becomes unreachable.
The DUC fixes this by updating No-IP’s DNS records automatically.
