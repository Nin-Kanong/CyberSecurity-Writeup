## Introduction to Evilginx2:
### What is Evilginx2?
Evilginx2 is a man-in-the-middle (MITM) attack framework used for phishing credentials and session cookies of popular websites. Unlike traditional phishing that uses fake login pages, Evilginx2 acts as a proxy between the user and the real service, capturing both credentials AND session cookies, allowing attackers to bypass 2FA/MFA.

### Key Differences from Traditional Phishing (like Gophish):
  - Captures session cookies, not just credentials
  - Bypasses two-factor authentication (2FA/MFA)
  - Uses real domain services as proxies
  - More sophisticated and harder to detect

| ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **config**        | Manage general configuration                                       |
